## Prerequisites

- Black Ops 3 from Steam or [Torrent](https://github.com/CBServers/client-dowloads/raw/main/torrents/t7_full_game.torrent)
- [boiii.exe](https://github.com/CBServers/updater/raw/main/updater/boiii/boiii.exe) file

## Manual Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the client">
    Download [boiii.exe](https://github.com/CBServers/updater/raw/main/updater/boiii/boiii.exe).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place in game folder">
    Place `boiii.exe` in your installed Black Ops 3 game folder (Steam BO3 or torrented BO3).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch the client">
    Open the client by double-clicking on the exe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for updates">
    All necessary files should download automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play">
    Hit the play button and have fun!
  </Step>
</Steps>
