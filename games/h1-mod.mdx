<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Launcher Install">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download the launcher">
        Download [cb-launcher.exe](https://github.com/CBServers/updater/raw/main/updater/cb-launcher/cb-launcher.exe).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Run the launcher">
        Double-click `cb-launcher.exe` to start the launcher.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Modern Warfare Remastered">
        Click on the **Library** tab and find **Modern Warfare Remastered**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Setup the game">
        Click the **SETUP** button and choose your install type:
        - **Existing install** - Select the folder where your MWR files are located
        - **Download game** - Select a folder to download MWR and choose content (Campaign, DLC, etc.)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait for installation">
        If downloading, wait for the game to finish. If you setup an existing install, click the **VERIFY** button to verify your files.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Play">
        Click **PLAY**, then select the mode you want to play and have fun!
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Manual Install">
    ## Prerequisites
    - Modern Warfare Remastered from Steam or [Torrent](https://github.com/CBServers/client-dowloads/raw/main/torrents/h1_full_files.torrent)
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download the client">
        Download [h1-mod.exe](https://github.com/CBServers/updater/raw/main/updater/h1-mod/data/h1-mod.exe).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Place in game folder">
        Place `h1-mod.exe` in your installed MWR game folder (Steam MWR or torrented MWR).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch the client">
        Open the client by double-clicking on the exe.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select game mode">
        Select the mode you want to play and wait for the client to launch.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Update when prompted">
        Select "Yes" when prompted to update the client and have fun!
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
