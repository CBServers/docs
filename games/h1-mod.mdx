## Prerequisites

- Modern Warfare Remastered from Steam or [Torrent](https://github.com/CBServers/client-dowloads/raw/main/torrents/h1_full_files.torrent)
- [h1-mod.exe](https://github.com/CBServers/updater/raw/main/updater/h1-mod/data/h1-mod.exe) file

## Manual Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the client">
    Download [h1-mod.exe](https://github.com/CBServers/updater/raw/main/updater/h1-mod/data/h1-mod.exe).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place in game folder">
    Place `h1-mod.exe` in your installed MWR game folder (Steam MWR or torrented MWR).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch the client">
    Open the client by double-clicking on the exe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select game mode">
    Select the mode you want to play and wait for the client to launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update when prompted">
    Select "Yes" when prompted to update the client and have fun!
  </Step>
</Steps>
