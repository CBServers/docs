## Manual Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the client">
    Download [iw6x.exe](https://github.com/CBServers/updater/raw/main/updater/iw6x/iw6x.exe).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place in game folder">
    Place `iw6x.exe` in your installed Ghosts game folder (Steam Ghosts or torrented Ghosts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch the client">
    Open the client by double-clicking on the exe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for updates">
    All necessary files should download automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play">
    Select the mode you want to play and have fun!
  </Step>
</Steps>
