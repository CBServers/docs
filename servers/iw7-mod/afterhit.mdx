</Info>

## How to Use

Type `/ah <weapon>` to set your afterhit weapon, where `<weapon>` is one of the weapons listed below.

**Example:** `/ah kbar-32`

You can preview the canswap by typing `/ah preview` after setting your afterhit weapon.
