These are weapons found in the game files that were cut or are glitched. They are available on the server through the `/cg` command.

## How to Use

Type `/cg <weapon>` to equip a custom gun, where `<weapon>` is one of the weapons listed below.

**Example:** `/cg r700`

You can save your custom gun so you spawn with it by typing `/lo save`. To clear your saved loadout, type `/lo clear`.
