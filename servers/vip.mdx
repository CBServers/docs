## How to Purchase

<Steps>

    Buy VIP through the [Discord Shop](https://discord.com/channels/841528688721788938/shop/1190189033930883143) ($10.99 USD including Discord fees). You must be on a PC to access the shop.

  <Step title="Set up your VIP">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Play">

  </Step>
</Steps>

Abusing the vote kick command to kick players who are not breaking rules will result in your VIP being revoked.
</Warning>
