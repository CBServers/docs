## How to Use

Type `/ah <weapon>` or `/afterhit <weapon>` to set your afterhit weapon, where `<weapon>` is one of the weapons listed below or any weapon in the game.

**Example:** `/ah kn-44`

You can preview the canswap by typing `/ah preview` after setting your afterhit weapon.

Afterhit supports **all weapons and specialist weapons in BO3** â€” just type the name of the weapon as it appears in the Create a Class menu.
